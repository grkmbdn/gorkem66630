Vacation App Project

Description

Developed a user-friendly vacation planning app to help users organize and enjoy their holidays efficiently.

Key Features

  • Destination Search: Find destinations based on interests and budget.
  • Itinerary Planner: Create and manage daily itineraries.
  • Booking: Integrated booking for flights, hotels, and activities.
  • Travel Guides: Access detailed guides and tips for destinations.
  • Weather Updates: Real-time weather forecasts for travel dates.
  • Social Sharing: Share plans and experiences with friends.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on all devices.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React Native.
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.
  • Database: MongoDB.

Outcome

Significantly enhanced user experience in vacation planning, leading to increased user engagement and positive feedback.
